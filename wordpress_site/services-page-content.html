Frequently Asked Questions

Why start with governance instead of technical controls?

Governance provides the strategic foundation that makes technical controls effective. Without proper governance, organizations often implement controls reactively, leading to gaps, inefficiencies, and 3-5x higher costs. NIST CSF 2.0 emphasizes "Govern" as the first function for this reason.

How do fixed-fee engagements work?

Fixed-fee pricing eliminates scope creep and provides budget certainty. After our discovery call, we provide a clear scope of work with defined deliverables, timeline, and price. No hourly billing or surprise costs.

Are your deliverables audit-ready?

Yes. Every document we create is designed with audit requirements in mind. We include evidence trails, accountability matrices, and metrics that satisfy both compliance requirements and operational needs.

What makes you different from other cybersecurity consultants?

Our governance-first approach, fixed-fee pricing, and rapid delivery timelines. Most consultants start with technology assessments. We start with strategic alignment, which reduces overall costs and accelerates compliance timelines.
